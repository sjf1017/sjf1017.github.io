Your Table by Personal Chef Lucas has an amazing team of staff members. We specialize in preparing and organizing Intimate Dinner Parties up to 30 guests. Romantic Dinners for Two up to 8 guests Chef Piper can prepare and serve alone. Then up to 10-13 guests it’s best to hire my excellent Kitchen Assistant/Server. We are a hit at every dinner party. When the guests reach up to 15-20 guests it’s highly recommended 2 servers are hired for the front of the house to help serve Hors D’Oeuvres and Dinner. For the Kitchen Staff it would be Chef Lucas, her Kitchen Assistant Oleg and definitely the Dishwasher/Kitchen Maintenance Staff member who is a life savior in the kitchen. We have a fabulous bartender when needed but of course our Servers can help pour wine or champagne.
